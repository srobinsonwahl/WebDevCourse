Tech House.


Metallic, light, dance.

Tech house is a subgenre of electronic music that mixes stylistic
features of techno with house music. In general, this style combines
musical aspets of techno like edgy baselines and metallic beats with the
grooves of house. It is a lighter style of music with less harsh beats
and more funky dance harmonies. The music originally had a clean and
minimal style that originated with techno from Detroit and the UK. It's
jazzy, captivating, and a vibe that could be seen as tranquil in the world
of techno. Since the early 2000's tech house has spread in Europe, with
major influence in the dance scene of Spain.
As a mixture of multiple genres, tech house's sound has to be a proper
balance of deep techno baselines layered underneath a melodic house hook.
Elements of the house sound such as realistic jazz-sounds and booming kick
drums are replaced with techno's deep and distorted kicks, noiser snares,
and more synth melodies. The sound is a middle ground between the two genres,
where techno's industrial tones are smoothed over with house's colorful
and peaceful tones. Some songs offer soulful vocals on top of the funky
beats to create a more peaceful sound. Tech house requires a mastery of balance.
