Techno is a genre of electronic music that never stops developing in sound.
Characterized by unweilded sound effects, hard-pumping baselines, and
hypnotic stand-alone rhythms, techno has made its way across hundreds of
countries. With its origins formed in Berlin, Germany, the techno scene
is very popular across eastern Europe, as well as Latin America.
